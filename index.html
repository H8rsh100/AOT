// Paradis Intelligence Archive — Chronological Record

Incident Report
~Year 1

Ymir Fritz makes contact with the Source of All Living Matter beneath a great tree. She gains the power of the titans — the first in all of history.

Origin Event
Historical Record
Year 1–1845

2,000 years of Eldian Empire rule using titan power. Marley eventually turns the tables, seizing control of the warriors program and titan weapons.

Classified
Military Report
Year 743

The Great Titan War. Marley seizes 7 of the 9 titans. King Karl Fritz retreats to Paradis Island with the Founding Titan. The Eldian Empire collapses.

Redacted
Wall Construction — Classified
Year 743

King Karl Fritz uses thousands of Colossal Titans to build three walls — Maria, Rose, Sina. He erases the memories of all Eldians within, fabricating a false history.

Memory Wiped
Incident Report — Shiganshina
Year 845

Fall of Wall Maria. The Colossal and Armored Titans breach the outer wall. Shiganshina falls. Eren Yeager witnesses his mother consumed by a titan.

Critical
Enlistment Record
Year 850

Eren, Mikasa, and Armin join the 104th Training Corps. The seeds of the alliance that will shake the world are planted. Later: Battle of Trost. Eren reveals his titan-shifting ability.

Monitored

Survey Corps expedition. The Female Titan is identified and unmasked as Annie Leonhart — a Marleyan Warrior who had infiltrated the 104th Corps.

Breach Confirmed

Marley arc begins. Eren infiltrates Liberio in disguise. The Raid on Liberio — Eren single-handedly attacks Marley and consumes the War Hammer Titan.

War Declared

Eren activates the full power of the Founding Titan. The Rumbling begins. 80% of humanity outside Paradis is wiped out. Eren is killed by Mikasa. The cycle of violence ends — or does it.

Powers at War

// Alliance Intelligence Files — Know Your Enemy

Paradis
Survey Corps

Humanity's scouts. Venture beyond the walls with the lowest survival rate of any branch. Erwin Smith and then Hange Zoë commanded. Levi Ackerman's squad. They carried the truth when no one else dared look for it.

"Dedicate Your Heart."
Paradis
Military Police

Serve the king inside Wall Sina. The most corrupt branch. Only the top 10 graduates earn entry — and most abuse it. Sheltered, privileged, and ultimately irrelevant when the walls came down.

"Guard the crown."
Paradis
Garrison Regiment

Defend and maintain the walls. The largest military branch. Most soldiers never see real combat. When the walls fell, many broke. A few, like Dot Pixis, stood and held.

"Hold the line."
Marley
Marley Warriors

Eldian children selected by Marley to inherit titan powers and serve as weapons. Reiner, Bertholdt, Annie, Zeke, Pieck, Porco. Promised honor and citizenship. Given trauma and impossible choices.

"For Marley. For the world."
Paradis — Extremist
Yeagerists

Eren's radical followers. Believe in Paradis' freedom through total annihilation of the outside world. Floch Forster led ground operations. Born from desperation, they became the thing they feared.

"Eren leads. We follow."
Marley — Noble
Tybur Family

Nobles who held the War Hammer Titan for generations. Controlled Marley's narrative about Eldians being devils. Willy Tybur's declaration of war on Paradis triggered the global alliance against Eren.

"We write the story of the world."
