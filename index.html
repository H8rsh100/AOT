The Three Walls

// They weren't protection. They were a prison. And the secret inside them changed everything.

Wall Maria
Outermost Wall — First Line of Defense
50m
Estimated Height

The outermost wall. Home to hundreds of thousands of people, including the Yeager family in Shiganshina. It fell in Year 845 when the Colossal Titan breached the outer gate and the Armored Titan shattered the inner gate. Humanity lost nearly a third of its territory in a single day.

Breached — Year 845
Wall Rose
Middle Wall — Refuge After Maria
50m
Estimated Height

Millions of Maria survivors flooded Wall Rose after the fall. Five years later, titans appeared inside it — transformed from Ragako village residents by Zeke's Beast Titan power. The wall itself was never breached, but humanity's sense of safety collapsed a second time.

Infiltrated — Year 850
Wall Sina
Innermost Wall — Seat of Power
50m
Estimated Height

The innermost wall. Home to the royal family, the Military Police, and the wealthiest Eldians. Most never saw a titan in their lives. When the truth came out — that the walls themselves were filled with colossal titans in hardened form — their entire world cracked.

Standing — Intact
Evidence — Item 001 — Shiganshina District
Grisha's Key to the Basement

Grisha Yeager wore this key around his neck every day and told no one what it opened. On the night Wall Maria fell, he pressed it into Eren's hands and begged him never to forget the basement. It took four years of war to reach it. What they found inside didn't just explain Grisha's past — it broke the entire foundation of what Paradis believed about the world. Memory. History. Identity. All of it was inside that one locked room.

Powers at War

// Every side believed they were the ones fighting for humanity.

Paradis
Survey Corps

They went where no one else would. Sixty percent casualty rate on expeditions. Most never came back. The ones who did kept going. Erwin built them. Levi held them together. Hange gave them purpose.

"Dedicate Your Heart."
Paradis
Military Police

The best graduates. The safest posting. The most comfortable lives inside Wall Sina. While the Survey Corps bled outside, they policed the capital and shook down merchants. When the walls fell, they had no idea what to do.

"Guard the crown."
Paradis
Garrison Regiment

Man the walls. Hold the line. Watch. The largest branch — and the one that saw the least real action. When Wall Maria fell, most froze. A few didn't. Dot Pixis looked at the end of the world and drank his wine.

"Hold the line."
Marley
Marley Warriors

Children. They were children when Marley picked them. Told they'd earn citizenship if they became weapons. Reiner. Bertholdt. Annie. Zeke. Porco. Pieck. They crossed the sea, buried themselves in enemy ranks, and tried not to feel anything.

"For Marley. For the world."
Paradis — Extremist
Yeagerists

They started with a cause. Paradis was going to be destroyed — they'd seen Marley's fleet. Eren gave them a reason to fight. Floch gave them permission to stop thinking. By the end, they were just another army doing monstrous things for a flag.

"Eren leads. We follow."
Marley — Noble
Tybur Family

The family that held the War Hammer for generations. They wrote the history books. They told the world Eldians were devils. Then Willy Tybur stood on a stage in Liberio and declared war on Paradis — knowing Eren was already in the crowd.

"We write the story of the world."

Critical Campaigns

// The tactical operations that changed the course of history.

Year 850 — Wall Maria
Return to Shiganshina
Basement Retrieval Operation

The ultimate gamble to reclaim Wall Maria and reach Grisha Yeager's basement. Facing a deadly ambush by the Beast, Armored, and Colossal Titans, Commander Erwin Smith led a suicidal cavalry charge to buy time for Levi to take down the Beast. Simultaneously, Armin sacrificed his body as a decoy to defeat the Colossal Titan, Bertholdt.

Outcome: Success / Reclaimed
Casualty Rate: 99.1% (9 Survivors)
Year 854 — Marley Empire
The Raid on Liberio
Declaration of War counter-strike

Following Willy Tybur's public declaration of war against Paradis, Eren Yeager initiated a preemptive strike, transforming in the middle of Liberio's internment zone. The Survey Corps deployed using advanced vertical maneuvering gear to support Eren, decimating Marley's military command and capturing the War Hammer Titan.

Outcome: Tactical Victory
Casualty Rate: Low (8 Scouts, Sasha Blouse)
